What is the shipping timeline?
Orders are processed within 24-48 hours. Domestic atelier shipping takes 2-4 days, while worldwide logistics takes 7-12 days via DHL Express.
How do I track my atelier drop?
Once your piece leaves the atelier, you will receive an encrypted tracking link via email.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns on unworn items within 14 days of receipt. Please ensure all original tags and packaging are intact.
